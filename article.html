Introduction to Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. These attacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information. As technology grows, so does cybercrime. Understanding basic security practices like strong passwords, secure networks, and safe browsing habits is essential for individuals and businesses. For beginners, learning about firewalls, encryption, and ethical hacking is a great starting point.

How to Secure Your Website

Website security is crucial for protecting user data and maintaining trust. Simple steps like using HTTPS, validating user inputs, and updating software regularly can prevent most attacks. Developers should also be aware of the OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities such as SQL injection and cross-site scripting (XSS). Using secure authentication systems and regular backups can save your website from major damage.

Beginner Guide to Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking involves testing systems to find vulnerabilities before malicious hackers do. It is a legal and professional way to improve security. Tools like Nmap, Wireshark, and Burp Suite are commonly used in penetration testing. Learning ethical hacking requires patience, practice, and strong knowledge of networking and systems.
